Builds the MPO that projects out the MPS component in a parity sector. |psi+-> = O |psi>= (1/2) (1 +- P) |psi> Here 1 = outer product of 2x2 identity matrices, "sites" times. Also P = outer product of 2x2 pauli matrices, "sites" times. The sign in "sector" and the factor 1/2 is put into the left edge at the end.
